---

## Step 1: Head to the game panel which can be found [here](https://panel.centrixnodes.net/)
<img
  src="/images/game/2fa/gamelanding.jpg"
  alt="Game Panel Landing Page"
/>


## Step 2: Login to the game panel and head to the "Account" section in the bottom left corner of the page.
<img
  src="/images/game/2fa/2falanding.jpg"
  alt="Game Panel Account Section"
/>

## Step 3: Click on the "Enable 2FA" button to start the setup process.
<img
  src="/images/game/2fa/2fasetup.jpg"
  alt="Enable 2FA Button"
/>

## Step 4: Follow the instructions to set up 2FA using an authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy). Scan the QR code or enter the provided key into your authenticator app.


<Check>
  **Congratulations!** If you've followed all the steps correctly, you should now have 2FA enabled on your account. This adds an extra layer of security to your game panel account, helping to protect your server and data from unauthorized access. Remember to keep your authenticator app secure and do not share your 2FA codes with anyone.
</Check>
