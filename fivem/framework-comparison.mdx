## Introduction

When setting up a FiveM server, You will be allowed to choose between several frameworks. There are several frameworks to choose from, each with their own advantages and disadvantages.

## Available Frameworks

<Card title="QBCore" icon="text-align-start" horizontal cta="Offical Website" href="framework-comparison#qbcore">
  QBCore is a framework that is designed to be easy to use and understand. It is a popular framework and is often used by beginners.

### QBCore
documentation: [QBCore Documentation](https://docs.qbcore.org/qbcore-documentation/)
#### Pros
- Feels easier for beginners.
- Comes with a lot of better maintained resources out of the box.

#### Cons
- Not as popular as ESX, so less resources are available.
- Not as well documented as ESX.

### ESX
documentation: [ESX GitHub](https://github.com/esx-framework)
#### Pros
- More popular than QBCore, so more resources are available and already made.

#### Cons
- Not as easy to use out of the box, if you are not familiar with FiveM.
